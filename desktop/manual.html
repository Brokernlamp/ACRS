Overview

AI Growth Operator is a desktop-first marketing analytics platform built for agencies managing multiple clients across Google Ads, Meta Ads, and other channels. Upload campaign data via CSV or sync directly from ad platforms, and the app instantly produces KPI dashboards, 7-day AI predictions, budget recommendations, and client-ready PDF reports.

📊

Live Dashboard

KPI cards, interactive Plotly charts, campaign breakdown table, and diagnostic insights — all in one view.

🧠

AI Growth Engine

7-day lead & CPL predictions, wasted spend detection, performance scoring, and a budget scenario simulator.

💬

AI Chatbot

Ask questions about your campaign data in plain English. Powered by Gemini with RAG over your actual data.

👥

Client Management

Add unlimited clients, track spend and leads per client, and manage platform account links.

📄

PDF Reports

One-click Standard and AI Growth Strategy reports. Email them directly to clients from the app.

🔗

Platform Sync

Link Google Ads and Meta Ads account IDs to pull campaign data automatically without CSV exports.

Quick Start

Get up and running in under 5 minutes.

1

Launch the app

Open AI Growth Operator from your Applications folder. The app starts its own backend and frontend servers automatically — wait for the splash screen to disappear.

2

Create or select a client

On the Dashboard, click + New Client and type a name, then press Enter. Or click an existing client button to switch to them.

3

Load campaign data

Choose Upload CSV and select your file, or choose Sync from Platforms and enter your Google/Meta account IDs. Click Upload & Analyse.

4

Explore your analytics

KPI cards, charts, and insights appear instantly. Navigate to AI Growth Engine for predictions and simulations, or AI Chatbot to ask questions.

5

Generate a report

Go to Reports and click AI Growth Strategy Report to download a PDF, or fill in the email fields to send it directly to your client.

💡 No data yet? Go to Settings → Sample Data and download the 60-day sample CSV to explore all features immediately.

CSV Format

The app accepts a simple CSV with the following columns:

ColumnRequiredDescriptionExample
dateRequiredDate of the data row2024-01-15
campaignRequiredCampaign nameGoogle Search
impressionsRequiredTotal impressions50000
clicksRequiredTotal clicks600
spendRequiredAmount spent320.50
leadsRequiredLeads / conversions45
revenueOptionalRevenue generated — enables ROAS5400.00
 
# Minimal example
date,campaign,impressions,clicks,spend,leads
2024-01-01,Google Search,50000,600,320.50,45
2024-01-01,Meta Retargeting,30000,180,150.00,12

# With revenue (enables ROAS calculation)
date,campaign,impressions,clicks,spend,leads,revenue
2024-01-01,Google Search,50000,600,320.50,45,5400.00
⚠️ Each row = one campaign on one date. Multiple campaigns per day is fine — just use separate rows. The date format must be YYYY-MM-DD.

Dashboard

The main view. Everything starts here.

Step 1 — Select a Client

Click any client button to load their data. Click + New Client to add one inline. The selected client is highlighted in indigo.

Step 2 — Load Data

Two options:

  • Upload CSV — choose a file and click Upload & Analyse. Data is saved to the database so you don't need to re-upload next time.
  • Sync from Platforms — enter your Google Ads Customer ID and/or Meta Ads Account ID, then click Sync & Analyse. Use the demo quick-fill buttons (Ajay, TechStartup, EcommerceBrand) to test with sample data.

Data Coverage Banner

Once data is loaded, a green banner shows the date range and row count stored for the client. If platforms are linked, a Sync Latest button appears to pull fresh data.

Date Filter & Comparison

Use the Start Date / End Date pickers to narrow the analysis window. The Compare To dropdown adds a period-over-period summary banner (Previous Week or Previous Month).

KPI Cards

Six headline metrics shown at the top of the analytics output:

Total Spend
Sum of all campaign spend in the selected period.
Total Leads
Sum of all conversions / leads generated.
Avg CPL
Cost per lead = Total Spend ÷ Total Leads.
Avg CTR
Click-through rate = Clicks ÷ Impressions × 100.
Total Clicks
Sum of all clicks across all campaigns.
Total Impressions
Sum of all ad impressions served.

Charts

  • Leads Over Time — daily lead volume trend line.
  • Spend vs Leads — bar chart comparing spend and leads per campaign.
  • Campaign Performance — multi-metric bar chart across all campaigns.

Campaign Breakdown Table

Per-campaign table with Impressions, Clicks, Spend, Leads, CTR, CPL, Conversion Rate, and AI Performance Score. Click Export CSV to download the table.

Wasted Spend Alert

If the AI detects inefficient spend, a red banner appears showing the total wasted amount, the worst-performing campaign, and the recoverable savings opportunity.

Diagnostic Insights

A list of plain-English observations generated by the analytics engine — e.g. "Google Search has the lowest CPL at ₹142" or "Meta Retargeting CTR dropped 18% this week".

AI Growth Engine

7-day forward intelligence. Navigate to AI Growth Engine in the sidebar after loading data.

Prediction Cards

CardWhat it shows
Expected LeadsPredicted lead count for the next 7 days, with trend direction and growth rate %.
Expected CPLPredicted cost per lead. Updates live as you move the budget simulator slider.
CTR FatiguePredicted CTR and whether a creative refresh is needed based on drop rate.

Recommended Actions

AI-generated action cards, each tagged with a type:

  • SCALE — campaigns with strong ROI that should receive more budget.
  • REVIEW — campaigns showing declining performance that need attention.
  • REALLOCATE — budget shift suggestions from weak to strong campaigns.
  • OPTIMISE — campaigns with high spend but poor conversion efficiency.

Performance Scores Table

Every campaign gets a score from 0–100 based on CTR, CPL efficiency, lead volume, and trend. The table also shows the AI-recommended budget and budget share % for each campaign.

Detected Patterns

A timeline of automatically detected patterns — e.g. day-of-week performance peaks, CTR fatigue signals, spend spikes, and improving/declining trends.

Scenario Simulator

Test budget changes before committing real money:

1

Select a campaign

Pick any campaign from the dropdown.

2

Set budget change

Drag the slider from -80% to +100%. The Expected CPL card updates live.

3

Run simulation

Click ▶ Run Simulation to see projected Spend Δ, Leads Δ, and CPL Δ. Click ⏸ Simulate Pause to see the impact of pausing the campaign entirely.

4

Print results

Run multiple scenarios across different campaigns. Click Print Simulation to download a PDF of all scenarios in the session.

AI Chatbot

Ask anything about your campaign data in plain English. The chatbot uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) — it has full context of your uploaded data, KPIs, predictions, and recommendations.

Requirements

  • Data must be uploaded on the Dashboard first (the chatbot reads from the indexed data).
  • A Groq or Gemini API key must be configured in Settings.
ℹ️ The green status bar at the top of the chatbot shows how many documents are indexed and which AI provider is active.

Suggested Questions

Click any suggestion chip to send it instantly:

  • Which campaign has the best ROI?
  • What is the current CPL trend?
  • Which campaign should I scale?
  • Where is budget being wasted?
  • What are the 7-day lead predictions?
  • Which day of the week performs best?

Token Usage

Each reply shows the token count (↑ input ↓ output) and the active provider. Chat credits are deducted per message — your remaining balance is shown in the top-right corner.

Controls

  • Clear chat — resets the conversation history. The AI starts fresh with no memory of previous messages.
  • Enter — sends the message.
⚠️ If you see "Chat credits exhausted", contact your account owner to top up. Credits are managed server-side and refresh automatically after a top-up on the next poll cycle (up to 30 minutes).

Client Management

Navigate to Clients in the sidebar to manage all your agency clients.

Client List

The table shows each client's industry, number of campaigns, total spend, total leads, and average CPL. Click Refresh to reload from the database.

Adding a Client

Fill in the form on the right side:

FieldRequiredDescription
Client NameRequiredUnique name for the client.
IndustryOptionale.g. SaaS, E-commerce, Real Estate.
Target CPLOptionalYour target cost per lead for this client.
Monthly BudgetOptionalTotal monthly ad budget.
Revenue per LeadOptionalEnables P&L calculation in Campaign Groups.

Deleting a Client

Click the trash icon on any client row. This permanently deletes the client and all their campaign data. This cannot be undone.

Platform Linking

From the Dashboard, when a client is selected and you choose Sync from Platforms, entering account IDs and syncing will automatically link those platform accounts to the client for future one-click syncs.

Reports & Email

Navigate to Reports in the sidebar. Data must be loaded on the Dashboard first.

Report Types

ReportContents
Standard Report KPI summary, campaign breakdown table, and diagnostic insights. Clean, minimal format suitable for internal review.
AI Growth Strategy Report Everything in the Standard Report plus AI predictions, recommended actions, performance scores, budget allocation, and detected patterns. Client-ready format.
Simulation Report Generated from the AI Growth Engine after running scenarios. Shows all simulated budget changes and their projected impact.

Downloading

Click either report button — the PDF downloads immediately. Reports are generated from the currently loaded data for the selected client.

Emailing a Report

1

Enter your Gmail address

The address you'll send from.

2

Enter your Gmail App Password

Not your regular Gmail password. Generate one at myaccount.google.com → Security → App Passwords. Format: xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx.

3

Enter recipient email

Your client's email address.

4

Click Send AI Growth Report

The AI Growth Strategy Report PDF is attached and sent automatically.

💡 Gmail App Passwords require 2-Step Verification to be enabled on your Google account.
